Jianlong Wu (吴建龙)

I'm Jianlong Wu, an associate professor and doctoral advisor in School of Computer Science and Technology, Harbin Institute of Technology(Shenzhen). Before that, I worked as an assistant professor in Shandong University from 2019 to 2022. I received my Ph.D. degree in computer vision from the ZERO Lab, School of Electronics Engineering and Computer Science, Peking University in 2019, advised by Professor Zhouchen Lin (IEEE Fellow) and Professor Hongbin Zha. In 2014, I received my bachelor's degree in electronics and information engineering from the advanced class, Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST).

Research Interest

Computer Vision, Unsupervised and Semi-supervised Learning, Multi-modal Learning.
